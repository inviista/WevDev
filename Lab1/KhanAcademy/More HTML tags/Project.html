Recipe #1: Fried potato

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 4 medium-sized
Oil 2 tablespoons
Salt To taste

Step 1: Wash and peel the potatoes.

Step 2: Cut the potatoes into thin slices.

Step 3: Heat oil in a pan and fry the potato slices until golden brown.

Step 4: Season with salt to taste.

Recipe #2: Omelet

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Milk 2 tablespoons
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste
Butter 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper. Beat well.

Step 2: Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.

Step 3: Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook until the edges are set.

Step 4: Flip the omelet and cook the other side until fully cooked.
